Welcome to the IEEE SMC AI-Powered Collaborative Autonomy Challenge (AICA). This page centralizes all important competition information and resource links.

The competition focuses on collaborative autonomous delivery using QCar2 (ground vehicle) and QDrone2 (aerial vehicle), with virtual and in-person stages.

📌 Overview

The IEEE SMC AI-Powered Collaborative Autonomy Challenge (AICA) is organized as part of IEEE SMC 2026 in collaboration with Quanser and the University of Texas at Arlington (UTA).

Virtual Stage: Teams implement and test solutions in Quanser QLabs and submit a complete mission execution video.

In-Person Stage: Top teams are invited to demonstrate their solutions live at IEEE SMC 2026.
