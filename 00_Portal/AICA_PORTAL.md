## Overview

~~The IEEE SMC (AICA) Challenge** is a competition focused on developing autonomous algorithms for **multimodal collaborative delivery** using the coordination of a **ground vehicle** and an **aerial vehicle**.~~

The **IEEE SMC AICA Challenge** is a competition focused on developing delivery plans and implementing path-planning and control algorithms for coordinating a **QCar2** (ground vehicle) and a **QDrone2** (aerial vehicle) within a **multimodal autonomous delivery system**.

The competition is conducted in **two stages**:

- **Virtual Stage** – Teams develop, implement, and test their solutions in Quanser Interactive Lab (QLabs) simulation environment based on the provided virtual stage scenario, and submit a video link demonstrating complete mission execution and system performance.

- **Conference Stage** – The **top teams** from the virtual stage will be invited to compete at the **IEEE SMC 2026 Conference (October 4–7, Bellevue, Washington)**. In this stage, teams will execute and demonstrate their solutions in the same **QLabs** simulation environment based on the Conference Stage scenario.
