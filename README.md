## Overview

The **IEEE SMC AICA Challenge** is a competition focused on developing delivery plans and implementing path-planning and control algorithms for coordinating a **QCar2** (ground vehicle) and a **QDrone2** (aerial vehicle) within a **multimodal autonomous delivery system**.

The competition is conducted in **two stages**:

- **Virtual Stage** – Teams develop, implement, and test their solutions in the Quanser Interactive Labs (QLabs) simulation environment and submit a video demonstrating complete mission execution.

- **Conference Stage** – Top teams from the Virtual Stage will be invited to compete live at the **IEEE SMC 2026 Conference (October 4–7, Bellevue, Washington)**.
