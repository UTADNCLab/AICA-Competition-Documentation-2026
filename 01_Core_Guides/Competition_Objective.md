# Competition Objective

This page contains one unified objective structure, with Virtual stage and In-Person stage subsections.

## Main Competition Objective For Both Stages

The objective of the AICA competition is to develop autonomous algorithms for collaborative delivery, where teams coordinate heterogeneous vehicles such as ground and aerial platforms to efficiently complete missions under realistic operational constraints.

For AICA, the mission focus is coordinated multi-agent delivery using **QCar2 + QDrone2**, with score and completion performance driven by delivery strategy and execution quality.
